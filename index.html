About Me

Four years behind the lens, and behind the timeline.

Sunny is a video editor and cinematographer working out of Punjab, India, who moved from freelance YouTube edits into full-cycle production — shooting, directing and cutting content for brands that need to earn attention on a six-second scroll.

The work spans product commercials, fashion campaigns and mood-driven cinematic shorts — always built around a clean visual pace, considered color grading and a story that lands before the viewer's thumb moves.
